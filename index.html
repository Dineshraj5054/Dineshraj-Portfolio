Dineshraj G

Instructional Designer • Learning Experience Designer • Learning & Development

I'm a creative instructional designer with 4 years of experience across edtech, educational institutions, and corporate L&D. I believe learning is a natural skill we begin honing from birth — it should be an effortless, dopamine-spiking experience. Somewhere along the way, schools and colleges turned it into a forced act. But it doesn't have to be that way. Learning can be interesting. It can be interactive. It can drive real results. The question is: how? That's where my Master's in Instructional Design, creativity in design and strategy, cross-industry experience, and software skills come together — to bring learning one step closer to the audience, and one step easier for the provider.
